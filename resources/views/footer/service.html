Himadri Limited

Under “Himadri Limited” and “Multipurpose Himadri Agro- Processing Co. Ltd”, we currently have 6 potato cold storages all in the North of Bangladesh, in Rangpur, Shibganj, Upson (Bogra), Battoli (Khetlal, Joypurhat), Jagannathpur (Thakurgaon), Komorpur (Birganj, Dinajpur). Future plan is to increase the number of cold storages in different locations of Bangladesh and also expand the product range of items that can be stored other than potatoes like some fruits and vegetables.

Ejab Alliance Limited
